Previous Workshops

Our workshop is a continuation of several previous ones on similar topics:

  • The 1st Scholarly Document Processing workshop, which was held on November 19, 2020, at EMNLP 2020.
  • The 1st SciNLP workshop, which was held on June 25, 2020, at AKBC 2020.

The first instance of SDP (SDP 2020) brought together two previous workshops focused on processing scientific documents:

  • The Bibliometric-enhanced Information Retrieval and Natural Language Processing for Digital Libraries workshop (BIRNDL), that has been running since 2016.
  • The Workshop on Mining Scientific Publications (WOSP), that has been running since 2012.
